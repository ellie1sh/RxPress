WELCOME TO RxPress

We are a team of developers transforming the way the system of pharmacies operate.

Why Choose RxPress?

RxPRESS is revolutionizing the pharmacy experience by streamlining customer interactions through QR code technology, which allows users to check in digitally and bypass long lines. Upon scanning a QR code, pharmacy staff receive instant notifications to prepare medications, significantly reducing pick-up wait times and enhancing overall efficiency.

QR Code Check-In

Skip the line with our convenient QR code check-in system. Scan the code upon arrival to notify pharmacy staff, ensuring a swift and seamless experience.

Say goodbye to long waiting times with our efficient QR code check-in system. Upon arrival at the pharmacy, simply scan the displayed QR code to alert the pharmacy staff of your presence. This streamlined process reduces wait times and ensures your visit is both swift and seamless. The system allows staff to prepare your order or service request in advance, making the overall experience faster and more convenient for everyone.

Diagnosis History & Transaction History

Patients' transaction history and diagnosis history will be at hand, allowing clients to conveniently monitor the specific type and quantity of medicine used in their purchase history.

Managing and monitoring medication records has never been easier. Our system provides patients with a detailed history of their medication purchases, including the type, dosage, and quantity of each item. This information is readily accessible to both patients and doctors. For doctors, it serves as a valuable tool to track compliance, adjust prescriptions as needed, and ensure proper medication usage. Patients, on the other hand, can effortlessly review their past purchases to maintain better control over their health and treatment plans.

Availability of Medicine

When stocks are unavailable, our app will show the nearest locations of pharmacies and branches that have the required medicines available.

Finding the medicine you need is now effortless with our app's advanced search feature. Users no longer need to visit multiple pharmacies in search of specific medications. Instead, the app provides real-time information about the availability of medicines across nearby pharmacy locations and branches. This feature not only saves time but also ensures you get what you need without unnecessary hassle, even in urgent situations.

Notification

Doctors and patients will receive different types of notifications. Doctors are notified of the medications required by their patients, while patients receive reminders to buy their meds.

organized with our comprehensive notification system designed for both patients and doctors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RxPRESS?

RxPRESS is a digital platform designed to streamline pharmacy experiences by allowing customers to check in and pick up medications using QR code technology. It reduces wait times, enhances efficiency, and improves customer satisfaction while ensuring secure handling of health data.

How does RxPRESS work?

RxPRESS works by allowing doctors to send electronic prescriptions directly to pharmacies, where medications can be prepared for pickup using a QR code. Patients can schedule pick-ups through the app, reducing wait times and improving convenience. .

How do I use RxPRESS to pick up my medications?

Simply log into the RxPRESS app, choose your pharmacy, and scan your QR code to pick up your medications.

How does RxPRESS improve pharmacy operations?

RxPRESS improves pharmacy operations by offering real-time inventory tracking, reducing prescription errors, and providing quicker medication dispensing. This leads to more efficient workflows and allows pharmacies to serve more customers in less time.

Can I get reminders for my medication refills through RxPRESS?

Yes! RxPRESS offers refill reminders to ensure you never miss a dose. The app will notify you when it's time to refill your medication, helping you stay on track with your health regimen.

Can RxPRESS be integrated with other pharmacy systems?

Yes, RxPRESS can be integrated with other pharmacy systems to ensure a smooth and seamless experience. This integration allows pharmacies to manage prescriptions, inventory, and customer interactions more effectively.
